I'm Dhruv, and this is my proof of life.


In high school, I had a mentor who always asked me about my proof of life - a story from the week that showed that I was doing something beyond the daily routine of classes and homework. It could be a funny story, a cool event, a good read, or anything that could be described as "not boring."

In college, I realized these proofs of life came about every day, so I started to document them on a calendar on my wall. But the end of the semester came around, and I had no easy way to store all those calendars and I figured by the end of 4 years of college, I'd have way too many calendars. So I decided to go digital. Here's a site dedicated to the crazy, interesting (or uninteresting) things that go on in life. Enjoy.
